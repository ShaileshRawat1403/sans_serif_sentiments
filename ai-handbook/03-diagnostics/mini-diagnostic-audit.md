# Mini Diagnostic Audit

Not sure why your last AI response went off track?

Use this **quick audit** to check for common prompt issues before you retry.

> _“Most prompt failures are clarity failures in disguise.”_

---

## ✅ The Quick Audit Checklist

Tick through this before hitting Send:

- [ ] **Do I have a clear objective?**  
  _What am I actually asking the AI to do or produce?_

- [ ] **Have I stated the format or structure?**  
  _Should the output be bullet points, a story, a table, an email?_

- [ ] **Did I give the right context?**  
  _Who is it for? What is it for? Any background or constraints?_

- [ ] **Is the tone or voice appropriate?**  
  _Is it supposed to sound friendly, expert, sarcastic, or formal?_

- [ ] **Have I defined what success looks like?**  
  _Length, tone, usefulness, or clarity expectations._

- [ ] **Would a stranger understand my prompt?**  
  _Is it self-contained and unambiguous?_

---

## 🪞 Self-Diagnosis Prompts (When the Output Fails)

Before rewriting from scratch, ask the AI:

```text
“What did you understand from this prompt?”
```

Or:

```text
“Why do you think your output didn’t match my goal?”
```

Let the model reveal the logic it followed — then refine your prompt accordingly.

## 🧰 Pro Prompt Audit Template

You can save and reuse this:

```text
Act like a prompt reviewer.
Here’s my prompt: 
---
[PASTE YOUR PROMPT HERE]
---

Now:
1. Identify its weaknesses (tone, scope, ambiguity).
2. Suggest 2 improved versions for different use cases.
3. Tell me how each version improves the output.
```
