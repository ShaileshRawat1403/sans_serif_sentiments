# Team Retrospective Prompts

Good prompt design isn’t just a personal skill — it’s a shared practice.

Use these team prompts during retrospectives, content reviews, or after major deliverables to reflect on **how you used AI** — and how you can improve next time.

---

## 🧩 General Reflection

- What did we try to do with AI in this task?
- Where did it help most? Where did it confuse or distract?
- Were we clear about _what_ we wanted or just _that_ we wanted help?
- What patterns in prompt failures did we notice?

---

## 🛠 Prompt Quality Checks

- Did we clearly define the goal in our prompts?
- Did we assign tone, format, and audience up front?
- Did we confuse the model with vague instructions or layered ambiguity?
- Did we over-explain or under-contextualize?

---

## 🎯 Output Evaluation

- Did the AI’s output match our expectations?
- If not, where did the gap occur — tone, content, logic, structure?
- Did we ask it to be something (e.g., expert, critic, writer) without giving enough detail?

---

## 🔁 Process & Iteration

- How many revisions did it take to get something usable?
- Did we iterate too much manually instead of adjusting the prompt?
- When did we give up and do it ourselves — and why?

---

## 🧠 Learning & Future Use

- What’s one lesson we’ll carry into our next AI prompt?
- What prompt format worked best for this use case?
- Should we save or template this version for future reuse?
