# 🧱 01 — Foundations

This module explores the philosophical and structural base beneath modern prompting, documentation, and communication. It covers:

- The purpose and psychology of written systems.
- The importance of shared language and glossary terms.
- The role of cognitive architecture and metaphor in meaning-making.
- The writing principles behind our unique communication style.
