# Change Communications Track

_A guide for change managers, internal communicators, and content teams to design empathetic, effective change messages._

---

## Modules

| Module                                                              | Description                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Anatomy of Change Communication](anatomy-of-change-communication.md) | Dissects the purpose, structure, triggers, and channels of every change message.                                                                            |
| [Communications Core](communications-core.md)                        | Presents a simple scoring framework to diagnose clarity, empathy, and impact in your change content.                                                       |
| [This Might Change Everything](this-might-change-everything.md)      | A comprehensive system covering why change communication must evolve, pacing (Slow → Slow → Fast), tone (Calm Clarity), facilitative formats, AI-awareness, ethics, templates, and operational workflows. |

---

## How to use this track

1. **Read the anatomy** to identify the building blocks of your current messages.  
2. **Apply the core framework** to diagnose gaps and score your content.  
3. **Work through “This Might Change Everything”** for practical templates, rituals, and integration steps.

Use this track to move beyond announcements and create change communication that truly _enables_ people to understand, adapt, and act.
